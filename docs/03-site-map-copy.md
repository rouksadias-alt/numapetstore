# Site Map And Copy

## Global Layout

Header:

- Left: brand mark and logo text.
- Brand mark: `N` inside a circle using primary brand color.
- Logo text: `Numapetstore` in a clean premium font.
- Menu: Inicio, Productos, Metodo Numapet, Resenas, Contacto.
- Right: cart button with item count.

Footer:

- Brand explanation.
- Links: Inicio, Coleccion, Sobre nosotros, Contacto, Politica de entrega, Cambios y garantia, Privacidad.
- WhatsApp contact.
- Delivery note: "Entregas contra entrega en zonas disponibles de Panama."
- Social icons placeholders: TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat.

## Homepage Structure

1. Hero

Goal: position brand as premium owner of the products.

Headline:

> La rutina premium para una casa mas limpia y una mascota mas feliz.

Subheadline:

> Numapetstore reune soluciones practicas para hogares con mascotas en Panama: menos pelos, agua mas atractiva y juego inteligente para el dia a dia.

CTA:

> Ver kits disponibles

Trust strip:

- Pago contra entrega
- Soporte por WhatsApp
- Oferta de lanzamiento
- Entrega en Panama

2. Problem section

Headline:

> Amar a tu mascota no deberia hacer tu casa mas dificil.

Pain cards:

- Pelos en sofa, ropa y cama.
- Agua estancada que tu mascota ignora.
- Energia acumulada cuando pasas el dia ocupado.

3. Product trio

Show all 3 products as a branded system:

- PeloCero Casa Kit
- AguaViva Flow
- JuegaSolo Motion Kit

4. Method section

Headline:

> El Metodo Numapet: limpiar, hidratar y estimular.

Explain the three pillars.

5. Social proof

Use placeholders until real proof exists:

- Review cards.
- UGC video placeholders.
- Before/after placeholders.

Label placeholders clearly in code comments so they are replaced before launch.

6. Offer section

Show bundle ladder and best-value logic.

7. FAQ

Questions:

- Como funciona el pago contra entrega?
- En que zonas entregan?
- Que pasa si el producto llega con problema?
- Como confirmo mi pedido?
- Sirve para gatos y perros?

## About Us Page

Purpose: authority and local trust.

Headline:

> Creamos Numapetstore para hogares panamenos que aman a sus mascotas, pero tambien quieren vivir mas tranquilos.

Sections:

- Why the brand exists.
- The Panama problem: heat, humidity, apartments, work routines.
- The Numapet method.
- Trust commitments: clear offers, COD, support, replacement policy.

## Contact Page

Fields:

- Name
- Phone
- Message

Also show:

- WhatsApp button.
- Delivery/confirmation hours.
- Support promise: "Respondemos lo antes posible en horario de atencion."

## Collection Page

Purpose: comparison and quick add.

Include:

- Product cards with star rating UI.
- Offer selector on card.
- Quick add opens cart drawer.
- Filters: Limpieza, Hidratacion, Juego.

## Product Landing Page Structure

For each product:

1. Hero with product image placeholder, rating, offer selector, CTA.
2. Pain agitation.
3. Product mechanism.
4. Before/after or demo image sequence.
5. What is included.
6. Why it costs more.
7. Social proof.
8. FAQ.
9. Sticky mobile CTA.

CTA behavior:

- Adds chosen offer to cart.
- Opens cart drawer immediately.
- Cart drawer shows cross-sells.
