# Design System

## Visual Direction

Numapetstore should feel premium, warm, modern and trustworthy. Avoid generic dropshipping aesthetics.

Design keywords:

- Clean home.
- Premium pet care.
- Warm Panama lifestyle.
- Soft but confident.
- High contrast CTAs.

## Colors

Primary brand color:

- Deep teal: `#0F766E`

Secondary:

- Warm cream: `#F7F2E8`
- Charcoal: `#111827`
- Soft mint: `#D9FBE8`
- Sand: `#EADCC5`
- Alert/urgency: `#B45309`

Use deep teal for:

- `N` circle.
- Primary CTAs.
- Active offer cards.
- Trust accents.

Use charcoal for:

- Text.
- Premium blocks.
- Cart drawer CTA if teal is already overused.

## Logo/Header

Header structure:

- Circle mark with `N`.
- Next to it: text logo `Numapetstore`.
- Use font like Inter, Geist, or Plus Jakarta Sans.
- Menu centered or after logo.
- Cart on the right.

Logo rules:

- No paw emoji.
- No cartoon logo unless brand later requests it.
- Keep text readable.
- Circle mark should look like a premium monogram.

## Typography

Recommended:

- Headings: Plus Jakarta Sans or Geist Sans.
- Body: Geist Sans or Inter.

Heading style:

- Large, bold, tight tracking.
- Clear emotional benefit.

Body:

- Short paragraphs.
- Spanish Panama copy.
- Avoid walls of text.

## Components

Required components:

- Header.
- Footer.
- ProductCard.
- OfferSelector.
- CartDrawer.
- CheckoutModal.
- UpsellModal.
- TrustBar.
- ReviewCard.
- FAQAccordion.
- ProductHero.
- SectionSplit with alternating image/text.

## Imagery

Use sample placeholders until real images are provided.

Placeholder requirements:

- Product pages: 3-4 image areas each.
- Homepage hero: one lifestyle image placeholder.
- Collection page: product card image placeholders.

Image section pattern:

- Desktop: alternate image left/text right, then text left/image right.
- Mobile: stack image above text.

Do not use random unrelated stock if it reduces trust. Use neutral placeholders with clear labels:

- "Imagen producto - reemplazar"
- "UGC video placeholder"
- "Antes/despues placeholder"

## Mobile

Most traffic is likely from TikTok/Snapchat. Build mobile-first:

- Sticky mobile CTA on product pages.
- Large touch targets.
- Cart drawer full-width on mobile.
- Checkout modal easy to close.
- Fast loading images.
- No heavy autoplay unless optimized.
