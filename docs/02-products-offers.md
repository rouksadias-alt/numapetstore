# Products And Offers

## Product Line

Numapetstore launches with 3 branded hero products. Use them everywhere: homepage, collection, cart cross-sells, product pages, trust sections, and post-submit upsells.

## 1. PeloCero Casa Kit

Core promise:

> Quita pelos visibles de sofa, cama, ropa y rincones sin depender de rodillos desechables.

Pain:

- Hair on sofa before guests arrive.
- Clothes with pet hair.
- Cleaning feels endless.
- Humidity makes odor and dust feel worse.

High-price framing:

- Sell as a complete cleaning system, not a brush.
- Include "Metodo PeloCero en 3 pasos": levantar, recoger, repasar.
- Add before/after visuals and user stories.

Product card heading:

> Casa lista en minutos, aunque tengas mascota.

Subheading:

> El kit premium para controlar pelos en sofa, cama y ropa sin complicarte.

## 2. AguaViva Flow

Core promise:

> Agua en movimiento para motivar mejor hidratacion diaria.

Pain:

- Pet ignores still water.
- Owner worries during workday.
- Bowl gets dirty fast.
- Panama heat makes hydration feel more urgent.

High-price framing:

- Present as a hydration station.
- Emphasize quiet flow, easy cleaning, and daily routine.
- Use "mas tranquilidad cuando no estas en casa."

Product card heading:

> Agua fresca que invita a tomar mas.

Subheading:

> Fuente premium para una rutina de hidratacion mas limpia y constante.

## 3. JuegaSolo Motion Kit

Core promise:

> Movimiento interactivo para entretener a tu mascota cuando estas ocupado.

Pain:

- Pet bored while owner works.
- Scratches, biting, anxiety-like behavior.
- Owner feels guilty.
- Apartment living gives less space.

High-price framing:

- Present as enrichment, not toy.
- Use "estimulo inteligente", "rutina de juego", "energia mejor canalizada."

Product card heading:

> Juego inteligente cuando no tienes tiempo.

Subheading:

> Movimiento automatico para mantener a tu mascota activa y curiosa.

## Offer Ladder

For each product:

- 1 piece: `$45`
- 2 pieces: `$65`
- 3 pieces: `$79`

Display the 3-pack as best value:

- 1 unit: "Para probar"
- 2 units: "Mas elegido"
- 3 units: "Mejor valor familiar"

The 2-pack and 3-pack should feel like the obvious choice:

- Show savings.
- Add "ideal si tienes mas de una mascota o quieres dejar uno en otra area."
- Use preselected 2-pack on product page.

## AOV Strategy

Cart cross-sells:

- If cart has PeloCero, cross-sell AguaViva and JuegaSolo.
- If cart has AguaViva, cross-sell PeloCero and JuegaSolo.
- If cart has JuegaSolo, cross-sell AguaViva and PeloCero.

Checkout upsell after valid lead submit:

- Show for 10-15 seconds.
- One click add.
- Only discount location in the funnel.
- Use USD.
- Recommended: `$19` accessory add-on or `$29` relevant product add-on.

Do not use `99 SAR` unless the store changes to Saudi Arabia.

## Scarcity And Urgency

Use honest scarcity:

- "Cupos de entrega contra entrega limitados por dia."
- "Oferta de lanzamiento para Panama."
- "Stock reservado solo cuando confirmas por telefono."

Avoid fake countdowns unless inventory or campaign windows are real.
